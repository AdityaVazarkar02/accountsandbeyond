Practical Accounting Course Syllabus

With Integrated Tally Prime + Excel Workflows for GST, TDS & MIS Reporting

Module 1: Accounting & Business Fundamentals

Objective: Build foundational accounting knowledge before automation with Tally & Excel
  • Basics of Financial & Cost Accounting
  • Journal / Accounting Concepts
  • Capital vs Revenue Transactions
  • Accounting Documents (Invoices, Receipts, Vouchers)
  • Accounting Cycle & Final Accounts Overview
  • Manual Entry vs Software Entry: Bridging the Gap
Learn: Build foundational accounting knowledge before automation with Tally & Excel

Module 2: Tally Prime Essentials (with Accounting Integration)

Objective: Develop practical skills in setting up and managing accounting data using Tally Prime
  • Tally Prime Introduction & Interface
  • Company Creation & Configuration
  • Ledger and Group Creation (Accounting & Statutory)
  • Voucher Types – Accounting & Inventory
  • Chart of Accounts in Tally
  • Bill-wise Transactions (Accounts Receivable / Payable)
  • BRS – Manual & Tally-based
Excel Activity: Design Chart of Accounts | Manual to Tally Reconciliation Tracker

Module 3: GST Accounting – Tally + Excel Workflow

Objective: Learn to record, reconcile, and report GST transactions effectively using Tally and Excel
  • GST Fundamentals (Intrastate vs Interstate)
  • GST Master Setup in Tally
  • GST Entries: Purchase, Sales, Credit/Debit Notes
  • GST Adjustment Entries
  • GST Reports & Return Summary (Tally)
  • GST Filing Prep using Excel Templates
  • Matching GSTR data in Tally & Excel
Integrated Task: Enter GST transactions in Tally → Export GSTR → Summarize in Excel → Compare with portal data

Module 4: TDS Compliance – Tally + Excel Workflow

Objective: Manage TDS deductions and compliance by integrating Tally entries with Excel reporting tools
  • TDS Sections, Thresholds, and Applicability
  • TDS Configuration in Tally
  • TDS Deduction Entries (Contractor, Rent, Professional Fees)
  • TDS Payable Ledger, Payment Vouchers
  • TDS Certificates and Reports in Tally
  • TDS Computation Sheet in Excel (Auto Calculation with Formulas)
Integrated Task: Tally Deduction → Export TDS Ledger → Generate TDS Summary & Form 26Q in Excel

Module 5: Payroll & Salary Accounting – Tally + Excel

Objective: Process and automate payroll accounting and salary management using Tally and Excel
  • Employee Master Creation in Tally
  • Attendance & Salary Structure Setup
  • Payroll Voucher Entries
  • Statutory Deductions: PF, ESI, PT
  • Payroll Reports in Tally
  • Salary Sheet in Excel (with IF formulas for allowances, deductions)
  • Salary Slip Automation in Excel
Integrated Task: Process payroll in Tally → Export to Excel → Generate Salary Slip MIS

Module 6: Inventory & Purchase/Sales Cycle

Objective: Control inventory and record purchase/sales transactions with Tally supported by Excel tracking
  • Inventory Masters (Stock Groups, Items, Units)
  • Purchase & Sales Orders
  • Inventory Accounting Vouchers (Purchase, Sales, Stock Journal)
  • Manufacturing & Batch-wise Tracking
  • Stock Summary & Valuation Methods
  • Reorder Levels, Godown Reports

Module 7: MIS Reporting – Excel Dashboards from Tally Data

Objective: Create dynamic MIS reports and dashboards in Excel by analyzing data exported from Tally
  • Exporting Tally Data to Excel (Day Book, Ledger, Trial Balance)
  • Creating PivotTables & PivotCharts for:
    • GST Analysis
    • Sales Summary by Region/Month
    • Debtors Aging
    • Expense Trends
  • Building Financial Dashboards
  • Ratio Analysis & Graphical Trends
  • Interactive Dashboards using Slicers and Timelines

Module 8: Year-End Processes & Auditing

Objective: Execute year-end adjustments and prepare audit-ready financial reports using Tally and Excel
  • Finalization of Books in Tally
  • Adjustments & Provisions
  • Depreciation Entries
  • Trial Balance – Excel & Tally Comparison
  • Audit Features in Tally
  • Auditor's Report Format in Excel

Module 9: Data Management & Utilities

Objective: Master data management and system utilities for efficient accounting operations
  • Data Backup and Restore Procedures
  • User Management and Security Settings
  • Import/Export Data Functions
  • System Maintenance and Optimization
  • Error Detection and Troubleshooting
  • Report Customization and Templates

Module 10: Capstone Projects & Evaluation

Objective: Apply integrated knowledge of Tally and Excel in practical projects and assessments for workplace readiness
  • Complete Accounting Cycle: Entry → Compliance → Reporting
  • GST Return Filing Project
  • Payroll Summary Project
  • MIS Dashboard Project
  • Final Practical Exam (Tally + Excel)

Module 11: Generative AI in Accounting & Finance

Objective: Leverage generative AI tools to automate, enhance, and streamline accounting processes
  • Introduction to Generative AI and its role in Accounting
  • AI-powered Data Entry Automation & Error Detection
  • Using AI to Generate Financial Narratives & Reports
  • Automating Reconciliation & Anomaly Detection with AI
  • AI-assisted GST and TDS Compliance Insights
  • Creating AI-driven Excel Models for Forecasting & Budgeting
  • Chatbot Integration for Query Resolution on Accounting Data
  • Ethical and Security Considerations of AI in Finance
Hands-on Task: Use AI tools to generate automated monthly financial reports and predictive analysis from exported Tally data combined with Excel
