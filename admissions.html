Admission FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our admission process

What is the minimum eligibility for admission?

+

The minimum eligibility is 12th pass or graduation in any discipline. We welcome students from all educational backgrounds who are interested in pursuing a career in accounting and finance.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connections with our industry partners.

What are the payment options available?

+

We offer flexible payment options including full payment, installment plans, and EMI options. Scholarships are also available for deserving candidates based on merit and financial need.

How long does the admission process take?

+

The complete admission process typically takes 2-3 working days once all required documents are submitted. We strive to make the process as quick and smooth as possible.

Can I change my course after enrollment?

+

Yes, course changes are possible within the first week of enrollment, subject to availability and any difference in course fees. Our counselors can help you choose the most suitable course during the initial consultation.
